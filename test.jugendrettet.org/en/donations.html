ongoing donations

With ongoing donations you can support us monthly, quarterly, semi-annualy or annualy. Ongoing donations help us planning rescue missions more confidentely. Ongoing donations do not have to be of high volume. You decide whether you want to give 10 € or 100 €.
Ongoing donations are provided via Betterplace. They end either when you end them or when we close the project on betterplace.org → for donations.

Just fill in the form below and decide for yourself how much and when you want to support us.
